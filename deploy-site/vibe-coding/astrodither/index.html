AstroDither, a webGPU & TSL experiment

AstroDither is a audio reactive TSL & webGPU experiment built with ThreeJS. This started after saw an online GIF i decided to recreate, trying to explore new tech like TSL with webGPU.
Nothing fancy, just goooood old R&D.

SOME RANDOM TECH

Despite what you think, a lot is happening here. There is a custom dither on the model only, needed to mix with a generic scene and a custom fluid built from scratch in a single pass for performances. On top of that a dither+displacement in postprocessing and selective bloom without masks, just fancy tricks. Oh wait, did you see the aberration?
